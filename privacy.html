隐私政策

最后更新：2026 年 7 月 8 日

BBCalc（波波汇率计算器）是一款汇率计算工具。我们尊重并保护你的隐私，本政策说明应用收集哪些信息、如何使用以及你拥有的权利。

我们收集的信息

  • 不登录即可完整使用：所有计算和配置（货币选择、排序、置顶、主题、语言）默认仅保存在你设备本地（localStorage），不会上传。
  • 可选的 Google 登录：若你选择登录以同步配置，我们通过 Google Firebase 获取你的昵称、邮箱和头像，仅用于账号标识与显示。
  • 云端同步的数据：登录后，你的应用配置（货币选择与排序、置顶列表、主题、语言、默认基准货币）保存在 Google Firestore，仅与你的账号关联。

我们不收集的信息

  • 不收集位置信息、通讯录、设备标识符；
  • 不接入任何广告 SDK，无广告、无跟踪；
  • 你输入的计算金额不会被上传或记录；
  • 不向任何第三方出售或共享你的数据。

第三方服务

  • Google Firebase（登录与配置同步），适用 Google 隐私政策；
  • 汇率数据源（Frankfurter/ECB、Currency API）：仅获取公开汇率数据，请求中不包含你的任何个人信息。

数据删除

你可以随时在应用的「设置 → 账号同步」中点击「删除账号」，这将永久删除你的账号及云端保存的全部配置。也可发邮件至 maxbobo007@gmail.com 请求删除。

联系我们

如对本政策有任何疑问：maxbobo007@gmail.com

Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 8, 2026

BBCalc is a currency exchange calculator. We respect your privacy. This policy explains what the app collects, how it is used, and your rights.

Information We Collect

  • Full functionality without sign-in: all calculations and preferences (currency selection, order, pins, theme, language) are stored locally on your device (localStorage) by default and never uploaded.
  • Optional Google Sign-In: if you sign in to sync settings, we receive your name, email and avatar via Google Firebase, used solely for account identification and display.
  • Synced data: after sign-in, your app preferences (currency selection & order, pinned list, theme, language, default base currency) are stored in Google Firestore, tied only to your account.

What We Do NOT Collect

  • No location, contacts, or device identifiers;
  • No ad SDKs — no ads, no tracking;
  • Amounts you type are never uploaded or logged;
  • Your data is never sold or shared with third parties.

Third-Party Services

  • Google Firebase (sign-in and settings sync), governed by the Google Privacy Policy;
  • Exchange-rate providers (Frankfurter/ECB, Currency API): only public rate data is fetched; requests contain no personal information.

Data Deletion

You can permanently delete your account and all synced settings at any time via “Settings → Account Sync → Delete Account” in the app, or by emailing maxbobo007@gmail.com.
